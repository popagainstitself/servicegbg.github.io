  • From the deep, spirited forests just below the Polar Circle, someone, or something, makes yearning sounds of loneliness on dew-covered spider web harps. Forest's music is a site for the sublime: finite individuality confronted by natur's overwhelming limitlessness, giving rise to an experience of the Absolute, That is why these sounds are utterly fragile yet comforting. Reconciling nature and subject, necessity and freedom, Forest’s music is art as organon – a means to express infinity in the finite. The artwork is playing with this romantic quest. One side is trompe l'oeil: on the vinyl record is actually printed an image of a vinyl record, allowing for the added twig and leaves. The other side is showing a forest from a painting found in the designer Alex’s family home. Truly enchanting. #SERV037 #forest #schelling #organon

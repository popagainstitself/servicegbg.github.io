  • From the deep, spirited forests just below the Polar Circle, someone, or something, makes sounds of loneliness on dew-covered spider web harps.

    In the face of the sublime, it is fragile and yearning, yet comforting.

    Reconciling subject and nature, Forest’s music is art as organon.

    The artwork is doubling this romantic quest. One side is trompe l'oeil: on the vinyl record is actually printed an image of a vinyl record, allowing for the added twig and leaves. The other side is showing a forest from a painting found in the designer Alex’s family home.

    Truly enchanting. #SERV037 #forest #schelling #organon Truly enchanting.

    Trompe l'oeil picture vinyl LP, limited edition of 250.

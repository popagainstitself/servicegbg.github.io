  • SERV004. This 7" was hailed single of the year by various people, and they were not wrong. Incredible artwork by Rasmus. Music and image blends into the dreamy and slightly spiked haze of early Embassy. You can almost hear producer Björn Olsson sleep under the mixing table, which he regularly did. This one began Service's jewellery heist era: several priceless diamonds would soon be collected.

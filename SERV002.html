• Studio - The Jungle/Shake You Down by the River

  • 1 The Jungle

  • This 7” was released at the second Hot Service party in the hangar at Valand University of Art. Studio made their debut stage appearance, which – possibly due to drug influenced time judgement – started at four in the morning. The palm tree could be seen spray painted on walls around Göteborg. Shake You Down... was later included on Studio’s West Coast album under the name Origin.

  • SERV012. Jens Lekman & José González.

    This split single is a manifestation of these great talent's sold out show together at the Göteborg symphonic concert hall, with just one Ep/album out between them. The concert was filmed and broadcasted by Swedish national television.

    Jens’ song is an invitation to anyone to have him sing at their wedding. His latest album is about his experiences from doing just that.

    Great artwork by Jens, printed on gold paper.

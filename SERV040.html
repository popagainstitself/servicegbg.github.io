  • Ikons' second album is inverting their hard rocking power kraut debut into a fountain of pulsating light. The unbreakable focus replaced by a dreamy gaze.

    Feet on lush ground, head in the clouds, Life Rhythm is a veritable candy box of shoegaze, jangle pop, laser guided melodies and dance floor psychedelica. I called it Swedendelica.

    The band was a powerful live experience and, fittingly, did some shows with Spiritualized.

    The artwork - which is corresponding with the debut's - was printed with a then new never before seen Pantone colour.

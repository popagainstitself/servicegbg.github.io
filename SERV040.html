  • Ikons' second album is inverting their hard rocking power kraut debut. Feet on lush ground, head in the clouds, the band's unbreakable focus is replaced by dreamy gaze.

    Life Rhythm is a fountain of pulsating light. A veritable candy box of shoegaze, jangle pop, laser guided melodies and dance floor psychedelica. I called it Swedendelica.

    Another testament to Service's amazing late era output quality.

    The artwork - which is corresponding with the debut's - was printed with a new, previously never seen Pantone colour.

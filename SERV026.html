• The Whitest Boy Alive - Burning/Inflation

  • 1 Burning
    2 Inflation
  • Double A-side single debut from the Berlin-based group led by Bergen-born cosmopolitan Erlend Øye.

    Their idea was to make analogue dance music with no filters or effects.

    A frustrated sound engineer once tried to explain to the band that you need to artificially manipulate the sound in order to create the appearance of original, non-manipulated sound; you need artifice to accomplish the natural.

    Here lied an involuntary crash course on the Derridean concept of différance. The band did not take notes.

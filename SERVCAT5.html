    Gloriously artificial, this is pop at its purest. Yet it’s die-hard DIY. Christina and Hanna had a rare and complete set of skills and did what few people can: they wrote, recorded, produced, mixed and performed.

    The result is as direct as it is diverse. Listen to album tracks like Slow Down and I Want You, and the pop house single Stretch and Bend. They still sound strikingly fresh.

    Their creative symbiosis is beautifully illustrated by Stefan Fält’s artwork.

    In order to maximize the album’s reach, Service licensed it to a major record company – an experiment not to be repeated.

    Is "Sexy" a kind of continuation of Right Said Fred’s hit, or a feminist critique using the very platform it criticises? National radio loved it either way.

  • Cat5 challenged expectations at the time, and magnificently so.

    This is laser guided pop at its purest, yet raw DIY. Christina and Hanna had an unusually complete set of skills, guaranteeing their vision and independence: They wrote, recorded, produced, mixed and performed their music.

    The result is as direct as it is diverse. Listen to tracks like I Want You, Slow Down and the pop house single Stretch and Bend. They all still sound remarkably fresh.

    Christina and Hanna deserve a life long adventure in music. Their creative symbiosis is beautifully illustrated by Stefan Fält’s excellent artwork.

    In order to maximize the album’s reach, Service licensed it to a major record company – an experiment not to be repeated.

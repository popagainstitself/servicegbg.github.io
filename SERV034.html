    The release was a conceptual play with the drastically transforming conditions for music distribution at the time. The predominant medium had shifted from CD to file sharing, while the transition to streaming had not yet taken off.

    We thought it fun to face our new lack of control and connection by reversing it in a rather aggressive way: We kept access to the music to an exclusive minimum, and engraved the monetary connection into the medium itself.

    * Transparent 12" vinyl.
    * Artwork by Carl Hammoud. Pigmented ink print on watercolour paper.
    * Buyer's name and price etched into the vinyl.
    * Certificate of authenticy.
    * Limited edition of three. (Plus three owned by artists.)
    * Sold by auction.

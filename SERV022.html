  • The concentration of talent in Göteborg during those years is astonishing. Cat5 had deep roots in the local scene. With such lightness did they wright, produce and perform pop songs, so that their first release had to be a double A-side.

    Is "Sexy" a semi-continuation of Right Said Fred’s hit, or a feminist critique using the very platform it criticises? National radio loved it either way.

  • Another conceptual yet highly danceable record from Service.

    Under his Kool Dj Dust alias, Daniel Savio digged every dusty crate(r), then molded the ultra obscure materia into 19 perfect disco rocks which he arranged dramaturgically into a galactic party drama. Visualized as a graphic story by Jimmy Gustavsson: The Disco Opera

    Dr. Sorryasscopycat has seized control over the city’s disco in order to control the people's minds and bodies by the most horrible means: lifeless music, oppressing everyone’s party spirit.

    The last hope stands to an ancient prophecy that a child will be born with the power to liberate the people. But the prophecy also speaks about a long-forgotten artifact, the Magic Record, without which this cannot be done…


    Of course, ultimately The Disco Opera is the story about itself.
    And its prophecy will be fulfilled only by YOU dancing to it.

  • SERV007. This Ep distributes aspects of Franke over three tracks: pop tune, heavy horror trip and ethereal beauty. Björn Olssons production is as radical, unique and masterful as Franke's songwriting.

    This recording is still available only on CD. Congratulations to those who experience the intensity of this dub goth pop power.

    The artwork by Ola with Artur Zarkowski, just like Franke's music, combines might and vulnerability. Made with stolen photo and found scrap, it is a successful anomaly in Service's catalog.
